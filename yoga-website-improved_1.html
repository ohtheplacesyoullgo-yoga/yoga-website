About Me

Welcome to Oh the Places You'll Go! My yoga journey began in college, during a time when life felt overwhelming and chaotic. I discovered that yoga offered me something precious—a way to drown out the noise of the world around me, to center myself, and to truly focus on the present moment.

As I continued my practice, I fell in love with the many different styles of yoga, each offering its own unique gifts. This deepening connection eventually led me to pursue my calling to teach. In August 2023, I completed my 200-hour Wellness, SEL, and Yoga teacher certificate through Breathe For Change, and since then, I've been sharing the practice with students and teachers alike in classroom settings.

For me, yoga is so much more than physical movement. It's calming and centering. It pushes my body to its limits while simultaneously helping me reconnect with nature and the magic of the elements. That's why you'll find my classes taking place outdoors, surrounded by the beauty of the natural world whenever possible.

My teaching focuses on movement and flow, weaving in intentional breathwork to help you feel centered and grounded in your own body. I offer classes in Vinyasa, Sculpt, Hatha, and Yin—each style bringing something different to your practice.

Everyone is welcome here—from young children to older adults, from complete beginners to experienced yogis. Yoga is for every body, and I'm honored to be part of your journey wherever you are in your practice.

Class Details

Class Styles Offered

  • Vinyasa: More movement, a quicker pace, and more flow work.
  • Sculpt: The same as Vinyasa but will include weights.
  • Hatha: Slower-paced but still has flow and focuses on breath control during movement.
  • Yin: Very slow, passive, and meditative. Yin yoga poses are held for much longer periods of time.

What to Bring

  • 🧘‍♀️ Yoga mat: Participants are encouraged to bring their own mat, but I have some that can be loaned out in case someone doesn't have one.
  • 💧 Water bottle: Everyone should bring a water bottle to stay hydrated.
  • 🏋️‍♀️ Weights (for Sculpt): If you are wanting to include weights in your practice, you must bring your own.
  • 🛏️ Blankets or pillows (for Yin): You may bring these for extra comfort during this practice.

Pricing & Registration

These classes are drop-ins—you do not need to register beforehand to participate! They are also "pay what you can" classes. I do not charge a fee for you to participate, but tips are always appreciated!

Who Can Attend

All ages are welcome! I ask that participants be respectful to me as a teacher, the people around them, and the space around them. Those not willing to show respect and kindness will be asked to leave.
