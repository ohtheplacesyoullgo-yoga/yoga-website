About Me

Welcome to Oh the Places You'll Go! I'm passionate about helping students explore the transformative power of yoga. Whether you're looking to build strength, find flexibility, or simply discover a moment of peace in your day, I'm here to guide you on your journey.

With experience teaching Vinyasa, Sculpt, Hatha, and Yin yoga, I offer a diverse range of classes to meet you wherever you are in your practice. From dynamic flows that challenge your body to restorative sessions that calm your mind, there's a place for everyone on the mat.

My teaching philosophy centers on mindful movement, breath awareness, and creating a welcoming space where students feel empowered to explore their potential. Each class is an invitation to connect with yourself, honor your body, and embrace the present moment.

I believe that yoga is for every body, and I'm committed to making the practice accessible and enjoyable for all levels—from curious beginners to seasoned practitioners.
